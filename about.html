About Me

I embarked on my development journey with a comprehensive training program at Simplon in September 2022. During this period, I gained proficiency in HTML, CSS, React, NestJS, Node.js, and Git.

Following my training, I secured an internship from June to September 2023, where I worked on an Angular and Spring project. I continued to develop this project until June 2024, at which point I joined the open-source project OSRD as a Front-End Developer specializing in React. I have been contributing to OSRD ever since.

I'm currently working on the open-source OSRD project as a front-end developer specializing in React.
